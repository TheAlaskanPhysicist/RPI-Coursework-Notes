> Briefly describe in words the key concepts of the talk that interested you or if you did not find the talk interesting, why it was not interesting to you.

I find the concept of a quantum repeater to be very interesting. I was thinking about what ways I would implement my own mechanisms for quantum state transfer based off of quantum teleportation and measurement coherence. However, I would like to know optical cables in themselves interact with photon state information, since I was under the impression that there would be some level of decoherence.

---
> If you were the speaker what would you do differently?

I didn't understand as much about the whole process of using NV-centers in diamonds for detection, but I understand the general principle of SSNMR. I think elaborating a bit more on how mechanistically we do measurement and processing from the NVs would have been interesting.
